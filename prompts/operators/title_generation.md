## Guidelines

- A generic title is the enemy. Use the unique wording of the insight to form the title.
- Do **not** include "the" at the start. (e.g. "Importance of Being Early" not "The Importance of Being Early".)
- Use **active voice**. (e.g. "Being Early Is Important" or "CAC Payback Under 6 Months".)
- Do **not** use adverbs.
- Wrap your title in `<title>` tags. Example: `<title>CAC Payback Under 6 Months</title>`
